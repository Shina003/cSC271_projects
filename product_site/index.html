Why Spendwise

Spendwise is designed to help you take control of your finances. With features that allow you to track your income and expenses from multiple sources, you will have a clear view of your financial health at all times.

Spendwise provides insights into spending habits, shows trends over time, and assists in setting and achieving financial goals.

Spendwise offers a user-friendly, comprehensive tool for tracking and managing finances. it is also accessible in design which ensures usability for a wide range of individuals

Key information the website will provide:

  1. Current balance
  2. Income, including breakdown
  3. Expenses
  4. Spending trends
